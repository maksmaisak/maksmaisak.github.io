# Job Search Workspace

This area stores private job-search material that supports the public portfolio site.

## Structure

- `facts/` — repository-backed source-of-truth notes about Maks's experience
- `applications/` — per-application working folders and generated deliverables
- `company-research/` — saved company and role research snapshots when needed

## Rules

- Treat `facts/` as evidence. Do not invent or embellish candidate claims.
- Put generated application artifacts under `applications/`, not in the public site root.
- The public site may summarize facts from here, but this folder is the deeper source material.
