Island Defence

In the third quarter of my first year at Saxion, as part of an introductory course in Unity3D, I was tasked with making a game to prove a basic level of expertise in Unity. To that end, I designed and implemented a first-person shooter with a variety of features, showcasing work with various systems of Unity, ranging from work with audio and physics to making an interactive tutorial and writing custom shaders.

The game received the maximum grade.

Overview

The game takes place on a small rocky island. The player has to use a gun and a shield to fight waves of hostiles parachuting onto the island. Health packs are continuously airdropped into the island, which forces the player to keep moving around the island.
